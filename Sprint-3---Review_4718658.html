Travel Booking System : Sprint 3 - Review

Created by Anushka Shetty on Jun 18, 2023

What went well? 

  • We created a feasible project plan at the beginning of the project and executed it successfully, ensuring timely completion of the project. 

  • Clear task division among team members prevented conflicts and enabled us to achieve our objectives effectively. 

  • Regular SCRUM sessions facilitated efficient and timely communication among team members, allowing for prompt issue resolution and progress updates. 

  • The positive customer satisfaction results from sprints 1 and 2 motivated team members to work harder during this phase. 

What should we stop doing? 

  • Arriving on time to SCRUM meetings is crucial to avoid making other team members wait and causing delays in the task schedule. 

  • Team members must promptly update any modifications they make to prevent ensuing issues. 

  • It's essential to keep SCRUM sessions brief and to the point. 

What should have been done better? 

  • Increased unit testing was performed on each feature developed. 

  • Timely and regular updates of Jira Board task status were maintained. 

  • Arrive at the meeting location 10 to 15 minutes early to ensure a timely start. 

What should we start doing? 

  • Team members should report any time or development-related problems they are experiencing, including the inability to complete a task within the allotted time range. 

  • To minimize unnecessary conversation during SCRUM meetings, team members should summarize their status and problems before the meeting.
